Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-14

This Privacy Policy explains how Arrival UK (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Arrival UK iOS app (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile details you provide: such as name, university, course, city, study level, and arrival date.
  • Checklist progress: completed tasks and custom tasks you create.
  • Device and app information: app version, basic device identifiers used for push messaging registration, and diagnostics.
  • Notifications: your permission status and preferences (for example, reminders and weekly digest).

2. How We Use Information

  • To personalize your checklist experience (for example, showing relevant tasks for your context).
  • To save and restore your progress across launches.
  • To send optional notifications you request (reminders, updates).
  • To maintain and improve the App, including debugging and reliability.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to provide functionality:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Functions, Messaging, App Check, Crashlytics): used for optional sign-in, syncing, push notifications, and crash reporting.
  • Google Sign-In: used when you choose to sign in with Google.
  • Advertising (optional): if advertising is enabled in your build of the App, we may use Google AdMob. When ads are disabled, tracking permission is not requested.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed to operate the App (for example, with the service providers above) and to comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the App and for legitimate business purposes, such as reliability and security. Some analytics events may have an automated expiration window.

6. Your Choices

  • You can edit your profile and reset local progress within the App.
  • You can control notification permissions in iOS Settings.
  • If ads are enabled, you can control personalized ads and tracking preferences inside the App and iOS Settings.

7. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at /delete-data.

8. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at support@arrivaluk.app.

Note: This policy should be reviewed and customized for your final production setup (especially advertising and analytics configuration).
