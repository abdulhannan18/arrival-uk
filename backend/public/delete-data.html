Data Deletion Request

Last updated: 2026-02-14

You can request deletion of your Arrival UK data by emailing: support@arrivaluk.app

Include:

  • The email address you used in the app (if any).
  • Your request: “Please delete my Arrival UK data.”
  • If possible, the approximate date you created your account.

What happens next

  • We’ll verify the request and delete associated backend records where applicable.
  • Local-only data stored on your device can also be cleared by signing out / resetting the app.

Note: Exact handling depends on whether you used sign-in features and what services are enabled in your build.
