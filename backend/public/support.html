Support

Need help with the app? Start here.

Email

Contact support at support@arrivaluk.app. Please include your device model and iOS version if you can.

Diagnostics

If you report a bug, include any relevant screenshots and the time it happened. In some builds we may also ask you to share diagnostic breadcrumbs from the app’s Help screen.

Emergency

If you are in immediate danger, call 999. For non-emergency medical help, call NHS 111.
